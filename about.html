About/ Tutorial Page

1. Click on the play button to start a new round
2. Countdown appears, enter your guess before it ends
3. Enter your guess in this input box. Only number 1-100 is allowed
4. Then click 'Guess' button to submit.
5. Your current guess will appear on this box.
6. Feedback will appear when countdown ends, providing the right direction. The first to guess the number wins and earns a score.
